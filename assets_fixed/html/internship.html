My internship

Here are the key skills and experiences I gained during my internship at Pixel Perfection, a dynamic web design agency dedicated to creating visually stunning and user-friendly websites:

NodeJS

I learned how to build scalable and efficient server-side applications using Node.js. This included understanding event-driven architecture, asynchronous programming, and utilizing frameworks like Express.js to streamline development.

Postman

I became proficient in using Postman for API development and testing. This involved creating requests, managing collections, and automating tests to ensure the reliability and functionality of APIs.

Sourcetree

I mastered Sourcetree as a graphical user interface for Git, enabling efficient version control and collaboration within development teams. I utilized features such as branching, merging, and visualizing commit histories to streamline the development workflow.

Team Collaboration

Working alongside talented designers and developers, I learned the importance of effective collaboration. I actively contributed ideas, provided constructive feedback, and leveraged collective strengths to achieve common goals.

Problem-Solving

Faced with various challenges throughout projects, I developed strong problem-solving skills. Whether it was troubleshooting technical issues or finding creative solutions to design constraints, I approached each problem with a methodical and innovative mindset.

Time Management

With multiple tasks and deadlines to manage, I sharpened my time management skills. I prioritized tasks effectively, set realistic timelines, and adapted to shifting priorities to ensure projects were completed on schedule without compromising quality.
